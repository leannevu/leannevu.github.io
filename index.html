    I'm Leanne Vu — a Computer Science and Software Engineering graduate from UW Bothell. Since graduating in August 2023, I’ve been on a self-directed journey to bridge development and data, starting with building full-stack projects using HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, and SQL. My goal is to combine strong technical foundations with product thinking to solve real-world problems at scale.
